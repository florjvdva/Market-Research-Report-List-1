<p><p>The bench-top sterilize equipment market consists of various types, including steam, plasma, hot air, and others. Steam sterilizers utilize high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms, making them effective for heat-resistant instruments. Plasma sterilizers use low-temperature hydrogen peroxide gas to sterilize heat-sensitive items without moisture. Hot air sterilizers function through dry heat to kill pathogens, suitable for items that can withstand high temperatures. Other methods may include chemical and UV sterilization, catering to diverse sterilization needs in laboratories and healthcare settings.</p></p>
