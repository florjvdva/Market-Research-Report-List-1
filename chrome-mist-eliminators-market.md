<p><ul><li>Wire Mesh Defogger</li><li>Baffle Type Mist Eliminator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chrome Mist Eliminators market includes two primary types: wire mesh defoggers and baffle type mist eliminators. Wire mesh defoggers use layered mesh structures to capture and coalesce mist droplets, effectively preventing liquid carryover in gas streams. Baffle type mist eliminators employ a series of baffles or barriers that disrupt airflow patterns, forcing mist droplets to collide and coalesce before draining away. Both types are essential for enhancing efficiency in various industrial processes, particularly in petrochemical and refining applications.</p></p>
