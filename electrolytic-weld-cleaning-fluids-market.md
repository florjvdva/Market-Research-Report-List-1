<p><p>The electrolytic weld cleaning fluids market is divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based cleaning fluids. Water-based cleaning fluids utilize water as a primary solvent, offering a safer, environmentally friendly alternative with effective cleaning properties. They are often biodegradable and less toxic. In contrast, solvent-based cleaning fluids employ organic solvents to remove contaminants and scales more aggressively, providing faster results but potentially posing environmental and health risks. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring quality and efficiency in metal fabrication processes.</p></p>
