<p><p>The Charging Interface Control Unit (CICU) market includes two primary types: DC Control Units and AC Control Units. DC Control Units facilitate high-power charging for electric vehicles, enabling quicker energy transfer and efficiency. They are typically used in fast-charging stations. AC Control Units, on the other hand, manage lower-level charging, suitable for home or slower commercial charging applications. Both units ensure standardized communication between the vehicle and charging infrastructure, promoting safety and interoperability in electric vehicle charging systems.</p></p>
