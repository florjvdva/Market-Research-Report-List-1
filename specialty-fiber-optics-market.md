<p><strong>The Specialty Fiber Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Singlemode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Specialty Fiber Optics Market is primarily categorized into two types: multimode fiber and singlemode fiber. Multimode fiber features a larger core, allowing multiple light modes to transmit simultaneously, making it suitable for short-distance applications like data centers and local area networks. In contrast, singlemode fiber has a smaller core and permits only one light mode, enabling higher bandwidth and longer-distance data transmission, ideal for telecommunications and long-haul networks. Both types cater to distinct applications within the fiber optics industry.</p></p>
