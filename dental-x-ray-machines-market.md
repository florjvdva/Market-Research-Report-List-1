<p><p>The dental X-ray machines market comprises mainly two types: ordinary X-rays and panoramic X-rays. Ordinary X-rays provide detailed views of individual teeth, roots, and surrounding bone structures, making them essential for diagnosing dental issues. In contrast, panoramic X-rays capture a comprehensive image of the entire mouth, including teeth, jaws, and surrounding tissues, enabling a broader assessment of dental health. Both types are crucial for accurate diagnosis, treatment planning, and monitoring oral health, catering to varied dental practices and patient needs.</p></p>
