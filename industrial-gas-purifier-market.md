<p><ul><li>Point of Use Gas Purifiers</li><li>Bulk Gas Purifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial gas purifier market comprises two main types: point of use gas purifiers and bulk gas purifiers. Point of use gas purifiers are designed for specific applications, ensuring high purity levels for gases delivered directly to equipment or processes. They are typically compact and installed at the point where gas is utilized. Bulk gas purifiers, on the other hand, treat larger volumes of gas before distribution, suitable for industrial applications requiring substantial gas quantities with consistent purity standards.</p></p>
