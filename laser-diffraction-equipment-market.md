<p><strong>The Laser Diffraction Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Particle Analysis</li><li>Dry Particle Analysis</li><li>Spray Particle Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser diffraction equipment is categorized into three main types based on the sample conditions: </p><p>1. **Wet Particle Analysis** involves dispersing particles in a liquid, ideal for analyzing aggregates and ensuring uniformity in the sample.</p><p>2. **Dry Particle Analysis** measures particles in a dry state, suitable for powders and bulk materials, allowing for quick assessments and minimal sample preparation.</p><p>3. **Spray Particle Analysis** focuses on analyzing particles in aerosol or spray forms, crucial for applications in pharmaceuticals and coatings to assess spray characteristics and distributions.</p></p>
