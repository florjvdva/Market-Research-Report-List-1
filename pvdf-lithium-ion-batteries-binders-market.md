<p><ul><li>Emulsion Polymerization</li><li>Suspension Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) lithium-ion batteries binders market includes two primary types based on polymerization methods: emulsion polymerization and suspension polymerization. </p><p>Emulsion polymerization involves dispersing monomers in water with surfactants to create a stable latex, yielding fine particles suitable for high-performance applications. </p><p>Suspension polymerization, on the other hand, creates larger polymer beads by dispersing the reaction mixture in a continuous phase, resulting in robust binders with excellent adhesion properties. Both methods are integral to enhancing battery efficiency and performance.</p></p>
