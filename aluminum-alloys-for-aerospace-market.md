The 6000 series, containing magnesium and silicon, is valued for its corrosion resistance and workability, suitable for components and fuselage structures. Foundry alloy ingots consist of various aluminum alloys tailored for casting processes, providing versatility in manufacturing aerospace components.</p></p>
