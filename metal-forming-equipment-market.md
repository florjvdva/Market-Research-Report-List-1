<p><strong>The Metal Forming Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Forming</li><li>Sheet Forming</li><li>Powder Metal Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal forming equipment market is categorized into three main types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves shaping metal in large sections using techniques like forging and extrusion, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Sheet forming focuses on processing thin metal sheets through methods such as stamping and bending, commonly used in automotive and appliance industries. Powder metal forming involves compacting fine metal powders into desired shapes, enabling intricate designs and reducing waste, often applied in manufacturing precision components.</p></p>
